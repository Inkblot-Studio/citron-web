## Private by design: the local-AI, privacy-first difference

Most "AI" business tools send your customer data to a shared model and hope you don't ask where it goes. **Citron takes the opposite stance.**

- **Your data is yours.** You own your Customer Data. Citron processes it only to run the service for you.
- **Never used to train shared models.** Your business data is never used to train models shared with other companies.
- **Private by default.** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, isolated per tenant, and protected by strict access controls.
- **Enterprise-grade control.** SSO, SCIM, advanced audit logs, custom AI models, and signed data-processing agreements for organizations that need assurance.

This is the whole point of a **privacy-first AI platform**: you get intelligence that acts on your business without surrendering ownership or control of the information that makes your business yours.
